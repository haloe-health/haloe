About Haloe

Healing rooted in the Sunnah

Hijama — wet cupping — is one of the oldest and most powerful forms of natural medicine. Endorsed by the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ and practiced for thousands of years, it draws out stagnant blood, toxins, and impurities to restore the body's natural balance.

Haloe is a women-only hijama practice based in Manchester, run by Halima Yasmin — a CMA and IPHM accredited therapist who graduated with an 88% distinction from The Suuk Academy's L5 Diploma in Health and Cupping Therapy.

Every session is private, dignified, and designed entirely around your comfort — whether you visit the clinic space or I come to you.
